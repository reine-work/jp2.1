Why should I register my business to JazzyPay?

Here are the top reasons why you should choose JazzyPay for your business to help make your decision-making process a lot easier.

Less fees, more growth

We don’t charge a setup fee, a monthly subscription fee, or a sales quota. Charges are only made for every successful transaction so we can further help your business grow.

Zero integration needed

Don’t have a website for your business yet? No worries, you don’t need website integration to start accepting payments online with JazzyPay!

Send payment requests online

Collect payments anytime and anywhere by sending bills and invoices to your customer via SMS or email through JazzyPay.

Referral incentives

We value our partnership with you, so we make sure to give back a reward if you share the word and encourage other businesses to upgrade their payment system.

Top notch security

JazzyPay is a registered Operator of Payments System (OPS) by the Bango Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP). We have also obtained the highest certification needed for digital payment systems, ensuring that you and your clients’ information are always kept safe and secure.

Open to innovation

We’re always looking for ways to make your user experience better, so we are always open to innovation opportunities and remain receptive to your feedback. Contact us anytime for suggestions and we’ll be happy to hear you out.
